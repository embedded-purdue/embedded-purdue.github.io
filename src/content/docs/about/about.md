**Embedded Systems @ Purdue (ES@P)** is a student-led organization at Purdue University dedicated to building a strong community around embedded systems, firmware engineering, hardware design, and real-time computing.

We aim to bridge the gap between academic theory and real-world engineering through **projects**, **workshops**, **industry talks**, and **collaborative opportunities**.

## Mission
- **Hands-On Learning**: Empower students to apply embedded systems concepts through hands-on technical projects.
- **Community Building**: Foster a supportive environment for students passionate about hardware and embedded software.
- **Professional Development**: Connect members with industry professionals, alumni, and research opportunities.

## Activities
- **Technical Workshops**: Learn skills like microcontroller programming, real-time operating systems (RTOS), low-level debugging, and PCB design.
- **Project Teams**: Join or lead projects that focus on building real embedded systems, from robotics to IoT devices.
- **Speaker Events**: Engage with guest speakers from companies like Amazon Robotics, Milwaukee Tool, and Garmin.
- **Competitions and Hackathons**: Represent Purdue at national competitions, or compete in internal embedded systems challenges.
- **Mentorship Programs**: Get paired with mentors for career advice, resume reviews, and technical guidance.

## Why Join?
- Build a **portfolio** of real-world embedded projects.
- Develop technical skills that are highly valued by employers.
- Meet like-minded students passionate about embedded engineering.
- Gain access to internal job opportunities, resume workshops, and alumni networking.

## How to Get Involved
- **General Meetings**: Open to all students, held bi-weekly during the semester.
- **Project Teams**: Applications open at the start of each semester.
- **Workshops and Events**: Posted regularly on our [Discord server](#) and [Mailing List](#).

> Whether you're an experienced engineer or just starting out, **Embedded Systems @ Purdue** is your home for building, learning, and innovating.
