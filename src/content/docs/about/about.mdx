## What We’re About

**Embedded Systems @ Purdue** is a student organization dedicated to hands-on learning at the intersection of hardware and software. Whether you're designing PCBs or coding for ARM microcontrollers, you'll find the resources, people, and projects to take your skills further.

### Our Mission

- **Hands-On Experience**: Learn by doing—build real-world embedded systems and electronics.
- **Community & Collaboration**: Connect with passionate peers and mentors.
- **Career & Research Support**: Prepare for industry roles and academic exploration through workshops, alumni, and guest speakers.

## What We Do

- 🧑‍🏫 **Workshops** — Learn microcontrollers, RTOS, debugging, and PCB design  
- ⚙️ **Project Teams** — Build robotics, IoT, wearables, and more  
- 🗣️ **Speaker Events** — Hear from engineers at Amazon Robotics, Garmin, Milwaukee Tool, etc.  
- 🏆 **Hackathons & Challenges** — Compete and apply what you learn  
- 👥 **Mentorship** — Get technical guidance and career advice

## Get Involved

We’re open to **all majors and experience levels**. You don’t need prior hardware experience—just curiosity and a willingness to learn.

- **General Meetings**: Held bi-weekly and open to all
- **Project Teams**: Applications open at the start of each semester
- **Workshops & Events**: Posted on [Discord](#) and the mailing list

> Whether you're new to embedded systems or looking to sharpen your edge, you'll find your place here.
