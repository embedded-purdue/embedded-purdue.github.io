## Mission

- **Hands-On Learning**: Apply embedded systems concepts through technical projects.
- **Community Building**: Connect with others passionate about hardware and embedded software.
- **Professional Development**: Access industry insights, alumni connections, and research opportunities.

## Activities

- **Technical Workshops**: Learn microcontroller programming, RTOS, debugging, and PCB design.
- **Project Teams**: Build real-world embedded systems, from robotics to IoT.
- **Speaker Events**: Meet industry leaders from Amazon Robotics, Milwaukee Tool, Garmin, and more.
- **Competitions and Hackathons**: Compete at national events and internal challenges.
- **Mentorship**: Receive career guidance and technical advice from experienced mentors.

## Why Join?

- Build a **portfolio** of embedded projects.
- Gain technical skills highly valued by employers.
- Connect with like-minded students.
- Access internal job opportunities and alumni networks.

## How to Get Involved

- **General Meetings**: Bi-weekly, open to all students.
- **Project Teams**: Apply at the start of each semester.
- **Workshops and Events**: Announced on [Discord](#) and [Mailing List](#).

> Whether you're experienced or just starting, **Embedded Systems @ Purdue** is your community for building, learning, and innovating.
