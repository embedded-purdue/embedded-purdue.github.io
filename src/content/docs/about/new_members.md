## [Page in Progress]


We’re **Embedded Systems @ Purdue (ES@P)** — a student-led organization building a strong community of innovators passionate about embedded systems, hardware, and software.  
We believe in hands-on learning, real-world projects, and connecting students with industry.

---

## Why Sponsor Us?

- **Top Talent Access**: Meet some of Purdue’s most motivated engineering students — future firmware engineers, embedded software developers, hardware designers, and more.
- **Brand Visibility**: Your logo on our projects, workshops, hackathons, and marketing materials. Get seen by hundreds of students and Purdue faculty.
- **Impactful Mentorship**: Help shape the next generation of engineers. We love when sponsors host tech talks, mentor projects, or just hang out with us at events.
- **Recruitment Pipeline**: Build early connections with students actively working on embedded projects — not just studying theory.

---

## Our Reach (and Growing!)

- 🎯 50+ active members
- 🔥 $6,000+ in funding secured in 2025 alone
- ⚙️ 10+ ongoing hardware/software projects
- 🎤 Frequent workshops and speaker series
- 📬 Mailing list, LinkedIn, and internal campus promotions

## How You Can Support

- **Monetary Sponsorship**: Help fund components, dev boards, competition travel, and workshop supplies.
- **Equipment Donations**: Microcontrollers, FPGAs, tools, dev kits — if we can blink an LED with it, we’ll love it.
- **Event Sponsorship**: Co-host a workshop, a mini-hackathon, or a "tech talk" night.
- **Recruitment Opportunities**: Share internships, co-ops, or full-time roles with our members.

---

## What You'll Get

- Featured logo and link on our website, flyers, and banners
- Shout-outs at events and on social media
- Resume books and early access to top students (optional)
- A direct channel to some of Purdue’s most passionate builders
